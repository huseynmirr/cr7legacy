Career

Neutral summary of club and international career. Sources listed at the end. Stats reflect public reporting as of Oct 2025.

Sporting Lisbon: 5 goals in 25 matches Real Madrid: 451 goals in 438 matches Manchester United: 145 goals in 346 matches Juventus: 101 goals in 134 matches Al Nassr: 106 goals in 119 matches International: 143 goals in 225 matches

Early Years & Sporting CP

2002–2003

  • Debut with Sporting CP first team.
  • Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira 2002.
  • His performance against Manchester United made him famous and he was ultimately signed by them in 2003.



Manchester United

2003–2009

  • Premier League titles 2006–07, 2007–08, 2008–09.
  • UEFA Champions League 2007–08.
  • FIFA Club World Cup 2008.
  • Won his first Ballon d'or there: 2008.



Real Madrid

2009–2018

  • Real Madrid's best player in history.
  • UEFA Champions League 2013–14, 2015–16, 2016–17, 2017–18.
  • Multiple scoring records in UEFA competitions.
  • Had multiple insane primes during Madrid era
  • Won 4 Ballon d'ors at Madrid: 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017


Juventus

2018–2021

  • Serie A winner: 2018–19, 2019–20.
  • Coppa Italia winner: 2020–21; Supercoppa Italiana winner 2018, 2020.
  • Named Seria A best player 2018-2019 and 2019-2020
  • Became the fastest player in Juventus history to reach 50 Serie A goals

Manchester United

2021–2022

  • Return to the Premier League.
  • European competition appearances and key goals.
  • Named Manchester United Player of the Year 2021–22.
  • Made PFA Premier League Team of the Year 2021–22.



Al Nassr

2022–present

  • Arab Club Champions Cup 2022-2023 winner.
  • Saudi Pro League Top Scorer 2023/24 with 35+ goals
  • Raised Saudi League’s Status: Helped attract other stars like Benzema, Neymar, Kanté, and Mané to Saudi Arabia.
  • Contract reported to 2027.


Portugal National Team

2003–present

  • UEFA EURO 2016 winner.
  • UEFA Nations League 2018-2019 and 2024-2025 winner.
  • All-time leading scorer in men's international football.
