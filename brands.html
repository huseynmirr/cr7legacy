Brands & Ventures

Brand partnerships and business ventures associated with Cristiano Ronaldo's commercial activities.

Endorsements

Cristiano Ronaldo has been associated with numerous global brands throughout his career, representing some of the world's most recognizable companies.

  • Nike: Long-term partnership as a global brand ambassador and signature boot collection
  • Watches: Partnerships with luxury watch brands including Tag Heuer
  • Fragrances: Personal fragrance lines and endorsements with major perfume brands
  • Consumer Brands: Various endorsements across fashion, technology, and lifestyle sectors
  • Sports Nutrition: Partnerships with fitness and nutrition supplement companies
  • Automotive: Brand associations with luxury car manufacturers

CR7 Brand

The CR7 brand encompasses various lifestyle and business ventures under Cristiano Ronaldo's personal brand umbrella.

  • Apparel & Lifestyle: CR7 clothing lines including underwear, sportswear, and casual wear collections
  • Hotels: Partnership with Pestana Group for Pestana CR7 hotels in major cities
  • Fitness: CR7 fitness centers and gym partnerships
  • Digital: CR7 mobile apps and digital platforms
  • Restaurants: CR7-themed dining establishments
  • Real Estate: Property development and investment ventures

Social & Media

Cristiano Ronaldo maintains one of the largest global social media followings and has appeared in various media productions.

  • Social Media: One of the most followed athletes globally across Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter
  • Documentaries: Featured in various football documentaries and biographical films
  • Series Appearances: Guest appearances in television series and entertainment programs
  • Advertising Campaigns: Starred in numerous high-profile advertising campaigns
  • Media Interviews: Regular appearances in sports media and mainstream press
  • Digital Content: Personal YouTube channel and digital content creation
