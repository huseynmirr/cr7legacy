Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on 5 February 1985 in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He joined Sporting CP's academy, made his senior debut in 2002, and soon transferred to Manchester United, where he won Premier League titles and his first UEFA Champions League.

In 2009 he signed for Real Madrid, becoming the club's all-time leading scorer and winning multiple Champions League and La Liga titles. He later played for Juventus, winning Serie A, and returned to Manchester United before joining Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia.

For Portugal, Ronaldo is the men's all-time leading scorer and most-capped player, winning UEFA EURO 2016 and the UEFA Nations League. Known for professionalism, longevity, and decisive goals, he remains one of football's most influential figures.
